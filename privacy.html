Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 06, 2026

Spider Solitaire Classic respects player privacy. This app is designed to work primarily with local gameplay data and does not require account creation.

Information We Collect

The app may store limited local data on the device, such as:

  • level progress
  • star ratings and best results
  • music on/off preference

This information is used only to preserve gameplay experience on the device.

How Information Is Used

  • to save game progress
  • to restore unlocked levels and ratings
  • to remember audio preferences

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not intentionally share personally identifiable information with third parties through normal offline gameplay.

Children's Privacy

This game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Stored gameplay data remains on the device unless the operating system or user chooses to back it up through Apple services.

Your Choices

You may reset or remove local game data by deleting the app or clearing the relevant app data through your device settings, where available.
